Raw results

Some of the raw results of the study can be downloaded in CSV format. Among other things, the table contains defect counts and time measured for each participant's session. The column "shortId" is the start of the ID you (hopefully) noted down at the end of the experiment. If you have further questions don't hesitate to contact me.

We only asked you to find correctness defects, but many people also remarked various flaws in the design and code style of the patches. We did not count these as defects for methodological reasons, so they are not contained in the above-mentioned table. Nevertheless, we generally agree to many of those remarks and would probably have remarked similarly in a real review setting.
